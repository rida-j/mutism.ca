The Selective Mutism Foundation (SMF) is a registered nonprofit organization with the mission to (1) collaborate on education, awareness and treatment for Selective Mutism; and (2) enable individuals to find their voice and overcome barriers.

The vision of the foundation is to be a respected leader in awareness, education and treatment for individuals who affected by Selected Mutism; and to provide a common ground where people with SM from all over the world can network and support one another.

Since SMF is a nonprofit organization (not a charity), we are not eligible for many, if not most, funding opportunities. Hence, your donation will assist us to carry on our mission, and continue to make positive impacts for those impacted by selective mutism.

Any amount is greatly appreciated!
